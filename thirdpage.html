The Philippines has plenty beautiful tourists spots. They are well known for their beaches, their mountains, and their history. The following places listed below are one of the most popular tourist spots found in the Philippines.



El Nido, Palawan

El Nido has been named a number of times by travel publication sites as the best island beaches in the world because of its paradise-like attractions with white sand, turquoise waters, vibrant coral reefs, and stunning limestone cliffs.

Chocolate Hills, Bohol

The Chocolate Hills of Bohol is one of the amazing tourist spots in the Philippines to offer a unique landscape, with its 1,268 cone-shaped hills that turn chocolatey brown during the dry season, hence its name.

Vigan City, Ilocos

Vigan, a 16th-century town, quietly boasts old-world charm and Spanish colonial architecture and one of the top historical landmarks in the Philippines.
